Altitude sickness can affect anyone. Prevention starts with the right itinerary and hydration.

## Prevention tips

- Ascend slowly and take acclimatization breaks.
- Drink plenty of water and avoid overexertion.
- Inform your guide if you feel headaches, nausea, or dizziness.
