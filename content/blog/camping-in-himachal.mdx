Camping in Himachal ranges from riverside stays to alpine meadows. Timing and gear matter.

## Best time to camp

- Spring and early summer for clear skies.
- Autumn for crisp weather and golden forests.
- Winter for snow camping (advanced, weather-dependent).

## Essentials

- Insulated sleeping bag
- Warm layers and gloves
- Headlamp and power bank
