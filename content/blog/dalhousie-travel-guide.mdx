Dalhousie is a perfect short-trip base with forest trails, meadows, and scenic drives.

## 2-day itinerary

- Day 1: Dainkund Peak sunrise + town stroll
- Day 2: Khajjiar meadows + Kalatop sanctuary walk

## 4-day itinerary

- Day 1: Dainkund Peak
- Day 2: Kalatop sanctuary
- Day 3: Khajjiar to Dainkund trails
- Day 4: Village walk or Chamba day trip

Pair this with a guided trek like [Dainkund Peak Trek](/treks/dainkund-peak-trek) or [Kalatop Wildlife Sanctuary Hike](/treks/kalatop-wildlife-sanctuary-hike).
