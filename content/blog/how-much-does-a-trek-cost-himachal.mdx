Trek pricing depends on duration, difficulty, stay type, and group size.

## Typical ranges

- Day hikes: ₹1,500–₹2,500 per person
- 2-3 day treks: ₹4,500–₹10,000 per person
- High-altitude passes: ₹12,000–₹18,000 per person

## What changes the price

- Private transfers vs shared transport
- Camping gear and meal inclusions
- Group size and guide ratio
