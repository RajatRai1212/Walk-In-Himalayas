## Route overview

Start from McLeod Ganj or Dharamkot, hike through forest trails, and camp at the ridge.

## Best season

March to June and September to December offer clear skies. Snowfall is possible in winter.

## Time required

Most trekkers complete it in 2 days with an overnight camp. Fit hikers can do it in a long day.
