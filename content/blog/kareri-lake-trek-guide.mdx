Kareri Lake offers alpine scenery with moderate difficulty, ideal for a 3-day escape.

## Highlights

- Cedar forest trails
- Alpine lake camping
- Quiet village stays

## Suggested itinerary

1. Day 1: Drive to Kareri village, acclimatize.
2. Day 2: Trek to Kareri Lake, camp overnight.
3. Day 3: Descend and return to Dharamshala.
