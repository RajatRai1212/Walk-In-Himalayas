Chamba Valley is where slow village life meets high mountain passes. As a local guide, I recommend mixing short cultural walks with one longer trail.

## Best season and planning tips

Spring and autumn are ideal for clear skies and stable trails. Summer is great for high passes, while monsoon needs careful planning.

## Suggested 3-5 day itinerary

1. Day 1: Explore Chamba heritage and acclimatize.
2. Day 2: Village walk and homestay.
3. Day 3-4: Trek to Bharmani Mata or Mani Mahesh (seasonal).
