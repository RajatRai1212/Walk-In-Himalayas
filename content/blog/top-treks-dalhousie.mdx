Dalhousie is perfect for short treks, family-friendly hikes, and scenic viewpoints. Here are the best local options.

## Easy treks

- [Dainkund Peak Trek](/treks/dainkund-peak-trek) – short, panoramic, and great for sunrise.
- [Kalatop Wildlife Sanctuary Hike](/treks/kalatop-wildlife-sanctuary-hike) – forest trails and wildlife spotting.
- [Khajjiar to Dainkund Trails](/treks/khajjiar-dainkund-trails) – meadows and gentle ridges.

## Best season

Dalhousie is open year-round. For snow, visit December to February. For clear views, choose March to June or September to November.

## Cost snapshot

Most day hikes start from ₹1,500–₹2,500 per person depending on group size and transport.
