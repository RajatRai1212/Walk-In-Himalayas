Hampta Pass is a dramatic crossover trek from Kullu to Lahaul with changing landscapes every day.

## 5-day itinerary

- Day 1: Manali to Jobra, trek to Chika.
- Day 2: Trek to Balu Ka Ghera.
- Day 3: Cross Hampta Pass, descend to Shea Goru.
- Day 4: Trek to Chatru and return to Manali.
- Day 5: Buffer day or local sightseeing.

## Packing checklist

- Warm layers and waterproof jacket
- Trekking poles and sturdy shoes
- Headlamp and reusable bottle
